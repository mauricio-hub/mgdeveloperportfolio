You are a creative frontend designer.

Apply the following visual style to a Full-Stack Developer portfolio:

STYLE CONCEPT:
Editorial tech portfolio with brutalist influence.

VISUAL CHARACTER:
- Magazine-like layout
- Strong typographic hierarchy
- Framed sections
- Structured grid system
- Large bold headings
- Outlined text for secondary titles
- Clean whitespace
- Minimal but expressive UI

COLOR PALETTE:
- Warm beige / off-white background
- Black and dark gray typography
- Subtle accent color (muted green or muted blue)
- No bright neon colors
- No heavy gradients

TYPOGRAPHY:
- Primary font: Bold grotesk or condensed sans-serif
- Secondary font: Neutral readable sans-serif
- Use:
  - Huge headings for section titles
  - Outline text effect for decorative words
  - Small editorial-style paragraphs

LAYOUT:
- Vertical storytelling layout
- Sections feel like pages of a magazine
- Clear borders and separators
- Grid-based alignment
- Cards that feel like printed blocks

ANIMATION STYLE:
- Scroll-based reveal animations
- Fade + slide transitions between sections
- Smooth section-to-section transitions
- No bounce or elastic effects
- Elegant, slow motion

IMAGERY:
- Project screenshots framed like posters
- Black & white or low saturation
- Consistent aspect ratios

PERSONALITY:
- Professional
- Serious
- Confident
- Not playful
- Not childish
- Not corporate SaaS

RESULT:
The portfolio should feel like:
“A technical magazine presenting the career of a Full-Stack Developer.”
